What is Planigale?

Planigale is a matching game using species from the Encyclopedia of Life. Planigale challenges you to match the picture of the species with its Latin name.

Latin name?

All organisms that have been discovered by science have been given a Latin name. These names follow a formal naming system first popularised by the Swedish botanist Carl Linnaeus called binomial nomenclature: the first word relates to the organism's genus grouping, and the second name to the species grouping. The information in the brackets that comes afterwards relates the the person that first described that organism, and in what year.

Who made this?
